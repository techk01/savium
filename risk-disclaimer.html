Important Information About Savings Accounts Accessed via Ledgernorth

Ledgernorth’s Role

LedgerNorth Group UK Ltd (consisting of LedgerNorth Group UK Ltd & LedgerNorth Management Ltd) (Company No. 14893472, trading as “ledgernorth.co.uk”) operates as a savings marketplace and facilitator. We provide customers with access to savings accounts offered by third-party financial institutions. While you may open and manage your savings account through the Ledgernorth platform, we do not hold deposits ourselves. All deposits are placed directly with the relevant bank, building society, or financial provider.

Deposit Protection

All savings accounts made available via Ledgernorth are provided by financial institutions authorised by the Prudential Regulation Authority (PRA) and regulated by both the PRA and the Financial Conduct Authority (FCA). Eligible deposits are protected under the Financial Services Compensation Scheme (FSCS) up to the statutory limit (currently £120,000 per person, per authorised institution). FSCS protection applies because your deposits are held with the underlying financial institution, not with Ledgernorth.

We act as a data controller in relation to the personal data we process.

Regulatory Status of Ledgernorth

LedgerNorth Group UK Ltd (consisting of LedgerNorth Group UK Ltd & LedgerNorth Management Ltd) is not itself authorised or regulated by the FCA because we do not accept or hold client deposits, nor do we provide regulated financial advice. Our role is to introduce you to FCA-authorised deposit-taking institutions and to provide the technology that enables you to access, view, and manage accounts. As such, we do not require authorisation to carry out our marketplace services.

Nature of Information Provided

All product information displayed on the Ledgernorth platform, including interest rates and eligibility criteria, is provided by the relevant third-party institutions. We make every effort to ensure that information is accurate and up to date, but we cannot guarantee availability or that the details will not change. Information on our platform is provided for general information purposes only and should not be considered financial advice.

Your Responsibilities

You should read the full terms and conditions provided by the relevant financial institution before opening an account. You are responsible for ensuring that any savings product you choose is suitable for your financial circumstances. If you are uncertain, you should seek guidance from an FCA-regulated financial adviser.

Liability

LedgerNorth Group UK Ltd (consisting of LedgerNorth Group UK Ltd & LedgerNorth Management Ltd) shall not be liable for any losses, whether direct or indirect, arising from: reliance on information provided on our Website or platform; changes made by third-party financial institutions; or your decision to enter into a contract with a third-party financial provider.
