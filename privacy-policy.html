About Us

LedgerNorth Group UK Ltd (consisting of LedgerNorth Group UK Ltd & LedgerNorth Management Ltd) is a UK-registered company (Company No. 14893472), trading as “ledgernorth.co.uk.” We operate a savings marketplace that helps savers find the best interest rates while protecting their finances.

ledgernorth.co.uk provides an online marketplace for savings products. We do not hold deposits or provide financial advice. Information provided is general and for educational purposes only.

Cookies and analytics
